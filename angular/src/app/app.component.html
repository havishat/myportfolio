About Me

Hi, I am Havisha. I am a self taught software engineer and learning new technology enthusiast. Interested in leveraging 4+ years of hands-on design experience in tech industry to pivot into software development.

I studied BS and MS in Electrical Engineering. My interest in computer science started while I was using software tools to implement hardware(ASIC) design. Due to this, I took online programming courses. I also attended coding bootcamp and I learned three full stacks (Python, MEAN and IOS) in 3 months. I have implemented multiple web and mobile apps by using various frameworks. I fell in love with coding and realized that programming skills are essential in our daily life.

I am a creative thinker with passion in building innovative apps while making the user's interaction as simple and efficient. I love to design and develop maintainable apps.

I love coding, traveling, singing (carnatic music), pencil sketching, volunteering, and blogging! Contact me if you want to know more about me.
